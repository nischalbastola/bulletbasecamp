The Bullet Basecamp Story

What started as a weekend passion project in 2004 has grown into the premier Royal Enfield adventure destination. Founded by motorcycle enthusiasts who believed that every rider deserves an unforgettable journey, Bullet Basecamp was born from countless miles on mountain passes, desert highways, and coastal roads.

The Beginning (2004)

Started in a small garage with three Royal Enfield Bullets and a dream to share the joy of motorcycle adventures. Our first tour took five riders through the Western Ghats.

Growing Passion (2010)

Expanded to a full-service motorcycle workshop and rental facility. Added professional tour guides and began offering custom adventure planning services.

Today (2024)

Over 10,000 satisfied riders, 50+ tour routes, and a fleet of 30 premium motorcycles. We're proud to be the trusted name in Royal Enfield adventures.
